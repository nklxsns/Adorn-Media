Additional Notice to Residents of the EU and EEA
The processing of personal data concerning persons in the European Union is governed by the General Data Protection Act (GDPR). Complete GDPR compliance is a priority for Adorn Media.
For general information on the types of personal data we collect and how your data is gathered, used, and stored, please refer to our privacy statement. Please refer to the material related to the GDPR below for further details.
What am I allowed to do under the GDPR?
With regard to your personal information, you have several rights. You can directly contact the data protection regulator in your country to find out more about your rights. Your rights consist of:

  • The ability to refuse processing
    You are entitled to object to certain processing, such as processing used for direct marketing.
  • The right to knowledge
    You have the right to get information that is unambiguous, intelligible, and honest about how we use your information and your rights (such as the information provided in this policy).
  • The right to access
    You have the right to ask to see your information as well as certain additional details pertaining to how it was collected and utilised. This request can be subject to a modest cost.
  • The right to be corrected
    If the information is incorrect or lacking, you have the right to have it updated.
  • The right to be forgotten
    You have the right to have your information erased in certain circumstances.
  • The option to limit processing
    The future usage of your information may be blocked or suppressed at your discretion. We can still store your information when processing is restricted, but we may not use it further. In order to ensure that the restriction is upheld moving forward, we maintain lists of people who have requested that further use of their information be blocked.
  • The right to data portability
    Your personal information is yours to collect and use anyway you see fit across various businesses. For instance, if you choose to change service providers, you may simply move, copy, or transfer your data between our information technology systems and theirs in a secure manner without compromising its usefulness.
  • The capability to file a grievance
    You have the right to complain to your country’s data protection agency about how we handle or process your personal information.
  • The ability to revoke consent
    You have the right to revoke your consent at any time if you have given us permission to use your personal information for any purpose (although if you do so, it does not mean that anything we have done with your personal information with your consent up to that point is unlawful). You have the option to revoke your permission for us to use your personal information for marketing purposes.

We have one month to respond to your request for information about your rights. You can use this page to exercise any of the aforementioned rights.
What legal basis do you use to handle my personal data?
Adorn Media LLP processes personal data in line with its (and its clients’) legitimate interests. If you’d like to see our legitimate interest assessment (LIA), we’re happy to provide it.
When you give us your information in order to use our products and/or services, we also process information with your consent.
Is the disclosure of my personal information required by law?
No
Is it a condition of the contract that I provide my personal information?
In order to deliver our goods and/or services to you, we might need some of your personal information, such as your name, contact information, and billing information.
We wouldn’t be able to provide you our goods and/or services if you choose not to give us your personal information as a client.
Do you use me for any automated decision-making or profiling?
Not at all, no.
Will my data be sent globally at any point?
When it is necessary, we may send your personal information outside of your country. The information is sent to our office in the United States using appropriate security measures in a safe and secure manner. When necessary, your data may also be sent to our offices in the UK, Romania, and Ukraine using the same secure and sufficient security measures.
